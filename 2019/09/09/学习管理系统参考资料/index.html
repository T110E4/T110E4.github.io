    Learning Management Systems: Ongoing Projects

    There are dozens of learning management systems in use today, representing a variety of business and development models. Below are some of the most well-recognized ones.

  • Canvas

  • Sakai (the basis for T-Square)

  • Moodle

  • Edmodo

  • Desire2Learn/BrightSpace

  • Google Classroom

  • Blackboard

  • Litmos
    For even more active learning management systems, see the Wikipedia list of learning management systems.
